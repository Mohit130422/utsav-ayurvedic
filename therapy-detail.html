    Abhyanga

    What is Abhyang Therapy

    Experience the soothing embrace of Abhyang therapy, where warm medicated oil envelops your entire body from head to toe. Say goodbye to muscle stiffness and pain as the healing properties of the oil work their magic. Its potent blend of herbs nourishes the skin, leaving it radiant and supple. But it's not just about the skin; Abhyang enhances joint mobility, making every movement more effortless. If you struggle with muscle tension or joint discomfort, Abhyang offers relief and relaxation, bringing you closer to a life of comfort and ease. The transformative power of this therapy will melt away tension, leaving you refreshed, energized, and deeply nourished, ready to embrace life with newfound enthusiasm and strength.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What are the different type of abhyanga?

    1. Sarvand abhyang-when full body is included.
    2. Ekaang abhyanga-on a particular body part.
    3. Shirobhyanga
    4. Padabhyanga

    What are the different oils used in Abhyanga?

    For vata-ksheerbala oil,mahamasha oil,balashwagandhadi oil etc.

    For pitta-any coconut oil base medicated oil like manjishtadi oil,eladi oil etc.These oils having cooling property which reduces excessive heat and burning sensation because of high pitta.

    For kapha-Oils made with sesame and mustard oil base suitable for kapha person like sahacharadi oil,marichyadi oil,karpooradi oil etc.

    Who should avoid Abhyanga?

    When someone has indigestion,early stage of fever or soon after fever,in high kapha condition such as cough with sputum,obesity etc,abhyanga is not recommended.Abhyanga is also contraindicated during the first three days or during heavy flow of menstruating women.

    What diseases can be treated with Abhyanga?

    Abhyanga can be done on daily basis to maintain overall health.It consider as a poorva karma(preparatory phase) of panchkarma.mainly it works on muscle stiffness,pain,dryness,skin tone,eyesight,sleeplessness etc.It helps to manage musculoskeletal and nervous system diseases.

    What is the difference between abhyanga and normal massage?

    Abhyanga employs holistic and integrative medicine principles used 3000 years ago in India, where it was first discovered. The main focus of this massage treatment is to balance out doshas within your body to achieve spiritual fulfilment and relaxation.In this different medicated oil is used for different body type.Its not just a deep tissue massage but this is also disease specific because of oil selection and Marma point to focus.
